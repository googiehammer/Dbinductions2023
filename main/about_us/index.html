RECENT WORKSHOPS

DATA SCIENCE


On March 5, 2023, a data science workshop was held as part of Vortex, the CSE department's symposium, targeting students from all batches. The workshop covered essential aspects of data science, including data collection, preprocessing, exploratory analysis, machine learning, and visualization. It emphasized ethics and offered practical skills for academic and professional growth, culminating in attendees receiving certificates for their dedication to enhancing their data science expertise.

FUNDAMENTALS OF DEEP LEARNING


An extensive workshop on 'The Fundamentals of Deep Learning' took place on April 14, 2023, targeting students from various years. The workshop covered key concepts such as deep learning principles, neural networks, training and optimization techniques, as well as specific applications like CNNs and RNNs for image analysis and NLP. Participants received certificates upon completion of the workshop.
